UII — Experimental Trial Management System

in progress

A real-time platform for managing and monitoring neurophysiological experiments. Supports two scenarios: prolonged monotonous tasks (Specialist and Commander workstations, EEG/ECG/GSR via LSL, real-time professional reliability forecast) and high physical load conditions (passive ECG monitoring with wireless transmission up to 1 km). Includes a digital specialist profile, HTML/PDF report generation, and a responsive interface.
